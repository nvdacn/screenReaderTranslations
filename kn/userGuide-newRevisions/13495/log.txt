    Abbreviate landmark names in braille (PR #6813)
    
    The names of "landmarks" are abbreviated in Braille
    
    Fixes #3975
    
    Abbreviations:
     - "landmark": "lmk"
     - "banner": "bnnr"
     - "complementary": "comp"
     - "contentinfo": "cinf"
     - "main": "main"
     - "navigation": "nav"
     - "search": "srch"
     - "form": "frm"
     - "region": "rgn"
    
    Example: "navigation landmark" becomes "lmk nav"
