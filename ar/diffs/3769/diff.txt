+==== Announce formatting changes after the cursor ====
+If enabled, this setting tells NVDA to try and detect all the formatting changes on a line as it speaks it.
+
+By default, NVDA will detect the formatting at the position of the System caret / Review Cursor, and in some instances may detect formatting on the rest of the line if it is not going to slow down NVDA's performance.
